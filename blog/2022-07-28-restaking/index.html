Restake support

By 3ventures

July 28, 2022

Hi stakers!, we’ve enabled restaking support for 3ventures validators in several networks:

Restake (you can click on the icons for each network to select which one you want to use), is a service that performs several tasks and provides some benefits for you:

  • Your wallet (Keplr for example) provides an authorization for the restaking operation, that is: just claim rewards on the validator and compound the rewards into staking, nothing else.
  • The validator, pays the fees for you, that is also ’extra’ money you save… as the restaking is paid by the validator itself.
  • The autorestaking grant is valid for a year, once it elapses you need to reauthorize the process.
  • During that period, and based on schedule (currently twice a day), your rewards when over the minimum amount, are claimed and restaked, generating compounded interests for your tokens.

All of this is based on the AuthZ support of the networks, but as we already added restake support, you can enable authorization for the 3ventures validator, and once the network itself supports it, will be handled automatically.

As of this writing we had it enabled for:

NetworkStatus
ChihuahuaAuthz not enabled yet

GenesisL1Operative

StargazeOperative

Remember, even if the network doesn’t yet support AuthZ, you can enable the restaking authorization and once it’s ready, it will automatically work for you… and in the meantime, you can use the interface to manually compound the interest.

Enjoy the staking done easy!
